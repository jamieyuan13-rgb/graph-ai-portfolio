About Me

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort course offered by GRAPH Courses. This program focuses on no-code AI tools for task automation, research, and scientific communication. Through interactive workshops, personalized feedback, and a capstone project, students develop practical AI skills that streamline work and enhance research workflows.

Course Modules

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations with AI
  • AI-Automated Data Analysis
  • Generative AI for Research & Text Analysis
  • Custom GPTs and Chatbots
  • AI for Task Automation
  • Generative AI for Communication & Outreach
  • Capstone Project & Showcase

Key features include weekly live workshops, access to a private learning community, lifetime access to tutorials, and a shareable certificate upon completion.
