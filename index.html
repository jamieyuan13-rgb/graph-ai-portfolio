I'm a first-year undergraduate student at UC Davis pursuing a Bachelor of Science in International Agriculture Development. My journey uniquely combines hands-on agricultural experience with cutting-edge AI learning.

This entrepreneurial experience has given me practical insights into inventory management, customer service, and business operations.

Through the Generative AI for Work & Research course, I'm exploring how AI can revolutionize agricultural research, data analysis, and international development communication.
