My Journey

I'm a first-year International Agriculture Development student at UC Davis with a unique blend of interests spanning organic farming, plant cultivation, and cutting-edge AI technology. As the owner of Plants4You, I've successfully cultivated and sold over 500 carnivorous and drought-resistant plants online.

My passion for sustainable agriculture and technology has led me to explore how AI can revolutionize agricultural practices and research. Through this Generative AI course, I'm discovering innovative ways to apply machine learning and automation to agricultural challenges, from data analysis to research documentation.

Course Modules

Module 1: Websites & Documents

LLM fundamentals, Markdown, LaTeX, HTML outputs, and AI presentations

Module 2: Data Analysis

ChatGPT Code Interpreter, Julius, automated dashboards and reports

Module 3: Research & Text

Literature reviews with Elicit, text classification, sentiment analysis

Module 4: Custom GPTs

Building chatbots, RAG systems, and research assistants

Module 5: Task Automation

AI agents, email automation, and complex workflow orchestration

Module 6: Communication

AI-generated visuals, video abstracts, and research dissemination

Module 7: Capstone Project

Final project development with peer feedback and presentation
